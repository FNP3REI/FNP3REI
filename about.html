About me

I’m Felipe Pereira, a Full Stack Developer transitioning from a 13-year career in the Aeronautic Industry to Software Engineering. Currently, I'm a Software Developer at Boeing, building engineering tools with C#, Angular, and SQL Server. With a background as an Electrical System Designer at Embraer, I’ve spent years solving complex problems in CAD applincations. Since 2019, I’ve shifted my focus to code—starting with C# macros, completing a B.S. in Computer Engineering in 2022, and now specialized in the MERN stack through MIT’s Full Stack Development program. My goal is to bridge the gap between robust engineering processes and modern web development, focusing on clean code, responsive UI, and efficient Git workflows.
